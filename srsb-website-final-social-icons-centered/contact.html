Candidate Safety

Important notice for candidates

SRSB Workforce Solutions Pvt. Ltd. does not charge any registration fee, interview fee, joining fee, placement fee or security deposit at any stage of recruitment.

Do not share money, OTP, bank details or personal documents with unknown people. For job-related confirmation, contact SRSB only through the official email, official website or company contact number.

Before you respond to any job message:

  • Verify the sender identity and company details.
  • Do not pay money for interviews or offer letters.
  • Do not share OTP, UPI PIN or banking passwords.
  • Confirm job communication through official SRSB contact channels.
