Company Overview

About SRSB Workforce Solutions

SRSB Workforce Solutions is built around a simple business promise: dependable workforce execution with clear ownership and support.

The company supports organizations with end-to-end workforce services across recruitment, staffing, payroll and employee lifecycle management. For employers, this means getting structured support from the time a requirement is shared until candidates are screened, interviews are coordinated, offers are closed and employees are onboarded.

For workforce operations, SRSB helps businesses manage activities that often become time-consuming for internal teams: documentation, joining formalities, employee query coordination, attendance follow-up, payroll inputs, PF/ESI support, F&F assistance and compliance reporting.

The goal is not only to fill vacancies, but to create predictable workforce support where speed, quality, compliance and continuity are handled together.

How We Work

A practical workforce delivery approach

SRSB connects hiring execution with employee operations so clients do not have to manage each step separately.

01

Requirement Discovery

We understand the role, location, experience level, skill requirement, hiring volume and timeline before sourcing begins.

02

Talent Mapping

Suitable candidate pools are identified through role mapping, screening and shortlisting based on the client requirement.

03

Hiring Coordination

04

Lifecycle Support

Support continues through onboarding, attendance coordination, payroll inputs, employee helpdesk and replacement support.
