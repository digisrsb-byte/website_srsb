Our Services

Recruitment, Staffing, Payroll and Workforce Management

Complete workforce support for businesses that need speed, quality, compliance and continuity in one operating model.

01

Recruitment Solutions

Recruitment support starts with understanding the client’s exact requirement: role, skill set, experience, location, budget, joining timeline and business priority. Based on this, SRSB maps suitable talent and shares screened profiles that are closer to the requirement.

Instead of simply forwarding resumes, the focus is on reducing interview effort and improving the selection ratio through role-fit screening and structured coordination.

  • Requirement intake and role understanding
  • Talent mapping and sourcing
  • Profile screening and shortlisting
  • Interview scheduling and feedback follow-up
  • Offer closure and joining coordination
02

Onroll Staffing Solutions

Staffing support is designed for clients who need workforce flexibility without creating a heavy internal HR operations layer. SRSB supports the employee lifecycle from offer release and documentation to onboarding, deployment tracking and employee support.

This model is useful when businesses need quick ramp-up, controlled workforce operations and continuity support.

  • Offer release and joining support
  • Document verification and employee setup
  • Onboarding and deployment coordination
  • Employee query and HR helpdesk support
  • Replacement support and escalation handling
03

Payroll Management

Payroll is handled as a structured monthly operating cycle where salary inputs, deductions, reimbursements, payslip coordination and reporting need accuracy. SRSB supports clients with payroll coordination that reduces administrative burden and improves compliance visibility.

The objective is to keep payroll predictable, documented and aligned with employee lifecycle changes such as joining, attendance, deductions and exits.

  • Monthly payroll input collection and validation
  • Salary processing and payslip coordination
  • Tax input and investment declaration coordination
  • PF / ESI and statutory deduction support
  • F&F settlement and payroll reports
04

Workforce Management

Workforce management connects hiring, staffing, payroll and employee support so that business teams have better visibility and fewer operational gaps. SRSB supports the everyday coordination needed to keep deployed teams working smoothly.

This includes attendance follow-up, employee records, documentation, lifecycle tracking, support queries and continuity planning.

  • Employee lifecycle management
  • Attendance and documentation coordination
  • Employee helpdesk and query support
  • Compliance tracking and reporting
  • Replacement and continuity planning
